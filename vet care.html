VET CARE TIPS

💉

Regular Vaccinations

Keep up with vaccinations to protect your pet from common diseases.

🩺

Annual Check-Ups

Visit the vet at least once a year for a thorough health examination.

🍖

Proper Nutrition

Feed your pet a well-balanced diet suitable for their age and health.

🏃

Exercise Routine

Ensure your pet gets enough physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

🐶

Parasite Prevention

Use vet-recommended flea, tick, and worm treatments regularly.

👁️

Monitor Behavior

Look for changes in appetite, energy, or behavior, and consult your vet if needed.
