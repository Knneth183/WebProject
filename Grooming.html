PET GROOMING TIPS

🛁

Regular Bathing

Use pet-friendly shampoo and ensure you bathe your pet once a month or as needed.

✂️

Trimming Fur

Regularly trim your pet’s fur to prevent matting and keep them cool.

🦷

Dental Care

Brush your pet's teeth with pet-safe toothpaste to maintain oral health.

🐾

Paw Care

Trim nails regularly and check for any cracks or dryness on their paws.

👂

Ear Cleaning

Check and clean your pet’s ears weekly to prevent infections.

🍎

Healthy Diet

A balanced diet helps maintain a shiny coat and overall health.
